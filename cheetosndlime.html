Cheetos and Lime

Prepare yourself for a zesty and spicy flavor explosion with this Lime and Hot Cheetos recipe. Combining the tangy brightness of lime with the bold crunch of Hot Cheetos, this dish is a delightful treat for those who crave a unique twist on their snacks.

Ingredients

  • Hot Cheetos
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Get your Lime
  2. Slice Lime in Half
  3. Squeeze onto Cheetos
