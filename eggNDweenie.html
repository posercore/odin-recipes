Weenie and Egg

Elevate your breakfast or brunch with this hearty and satisfying Hot Dog and Eggs recipe. Combining the savory goodness of hot dogs with the wholesome protein of eggs, this dish is perfect for those looking to start their day with a flavorful twist.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dogs
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cut up Weenie
  2. Cook to liking
  3. Add eggs and cook to liking
